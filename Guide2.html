The little wink game

This game was built around an idea that was born a few months ago: each level of the game will make a more or less important reference to one of the emblematic games from the beginning of video games.

Our game begins in a strange warehouse where we see in the dark, the hero of our adventure waking up. We can now leave the room. The next room, a laboratory room as strange as lastone , hosts a strange mechanism. Some will recognize it, its movement makes us think of Pong. This is where our second friend joins us. From there, the goal will be to manage to escape from the corridors of the laboratory and then flee through the streets without getting caught. If you get there, the true identity of our hero will be revealed and you can now travel with Patapouf in an unknown world...
A whole new era!
